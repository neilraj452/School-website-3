My school name is KERALA SAMAJAM MODEL SCHOOL

ABOUT

Kerala Samajam Model School (KSMS), Jamshedpur was started in 1981. The school is affiliated to the Council for ISC Examination, New Delhi. It is an English Medium co-educational institution preparing students for the ICSE & ISC Examinations. The school is managed by a Board of Trustees, comprising officials of Kerala Samajam, Jamshedpur. In this industrial city, Keralites are a Iinguistic minority. The School is dedicated to the benefit of all students irrespective of linguistic, social or religious considerations. The School educates students from all over the country. KSMS receives no government or private aid. Student fees are the only source of revenue. All students wear uniforms, which vary by grade level.
